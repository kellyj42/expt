Frequently Asked Questions


Have questions about EduResource Hub? We’ve got answers! Explore our FAQ section to learn more about how to use the platform.

Questions

How do I create an account on EduResource Hub?

▼

To create an account, visit the EduResource Hub website and click on "Sign Up". Enter your email or student ID, create a password, and verify your email address to activate your account.

How do I upload resources?

▼

Click on "Upload" to share educational resources. Select the file (PDF, MP4, MP3, etc.), add details like subject, course, and year, and ensure the file size is within the limit (e.g., 100MB).

How do I search for resources?

▼

Use the search bar to find resources by subject, course, year, or resource type (e.g., past papers, videos, audio files). You can also apply filters to sort resources by most downloaded, recent, or highest-rated.

How do I participate in the discussion forum?

▼

Go to the Discussion Forum from the main menu. You can browse through topics, start a new discussion by clicking "New Topic", or reply to existing threads.
